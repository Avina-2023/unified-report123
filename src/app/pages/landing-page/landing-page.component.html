{{'Skills Exchange'}}
L&T EduTech’s Skills Exchange is a platform for students and recent graduates to meet India’s premiere employers and vice versa. New graduates—students who are about to take their course-end examinations and recent graduates with up to two years of experience—can create customised profiles to showcase their talent, skillsets, credentials and assessment scores to prominent employers on the platform. Based on their profiles and the specific requirements of a job, these aspirants are further selected and shortlisted for the hiring process by employers.

The platform is also ideal for corporates looking to engage and employ skilled, talented, pre-assessed and job-ready candidates. Exclusive filtering is offered for corporates to get candidates vetted by L&T via its intensive courses and rigorous assessments.
