A BEI questions are standard interview questions to assess whether the candidates demonstrate these critical traits that fit the workplace culture. It is helpful to customize questions to align the requirements of the open position and the organization’s culture. Follow-up probes questions should be used to further explore the candidate’s response to a particular question.
